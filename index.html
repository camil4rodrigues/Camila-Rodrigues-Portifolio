Artista multimídia e designer nascida e criada em São Paulo, Brasil. Atualmente cursando design pela FMU FIAMFAAM.

With a background in fine arts and a keen eye for detail, I approach each photography session as an opportunity to create art. Whether I'm capturing the joy and connection of a family, the raw emotions of a wedding day, or the beauty of nature in landscape photography, my goal is to evoke a sense of wonder and create images that resonate with viewers.

I specialize in photography. Through my lens, I strive to capture the essence of my subjects, whether it's their unique personality, the ambiance of a specific location, or the interplay of light and shadow.
